Feature Overview

1. Patient Management

  • Patient registration (OPD, IPD, Emergency)
  • Demographics, contact, family history
  • Appointment booking (walk-in, call, online portal)
  • Appointment reminders (SMS/Email)
  • Queue management
  • Visit history with timeline
  • Case sheets and past treatments
  • Referral tracking (internal or external)
  • Patient ID / Barcode / MRN generator
  • Consent form management

2. Doctor & Staff Management

  • Doctor profiles (specialty, availability, schedules)
  • Calendar integration
  • Staff profile (nurses, technicians, admin staff)
  • Role-based access & duties
  • Shift & Rota management
  • Leave/holiday tracking
  • Digital signature for prescriptions/reports
  • Payroll tracking (basic for HR integration)
  • Task assignments (to nurses, assistants)
  • Internal communication (chat or notification system)

3. Electronic Medical Records (EMR) / EHR

  • Medical history (chronic diseases, surgeries)
  • Current visit details
  • Vitals monitoring (BP, glucose, etc.)
  • Clinical notes / SOAP notes
  • Diagnosis and ICD-10 codes
  • Prescription generator (drug name, dose, frequency)
  • Prescription printing / digital sharing
  • Document uploads (X-rays, ECG, lab results)
  • Vaccination records
  • Allergies / alerts

4. Billing & Invoicing

  • Service-wise charge setup (consultation, lab, surgery, room)
  • Insurance claim generation and tracking
  • Real-time billing updates (based on services used)
  • TPA integration (Third Party Administrators)
  • Refund processing
  • Discount and waiver management
  • Payment gateway integration (POS, online)
  • Tax and GST calculation

5. Pharmacy Management

  • Drug catalog (with dosage, form, stock)
  • Stock-in/stock-out entries
  • Batch & expiry tracking
  • Barcode scanning
  • Automatic low-stock alerts
  • Prescription-to-pharmacy flow
  • Billing from pharmacy counter
  • Vendor and purchase order management
  • Drug return and damage log

6. Laboratory Management

  • Lab test catalogue with categories (blood, urine, imaging)
  • Test order creation (manual and from doctor)
  • Sample collection tracking
  • Lab workflow: collected → processing → result ready
  • Report format templates
  • Result entry (with reference ranges)
  • Auto-flagging abnormal results
  • Digital report delivery to doctors/patients
  • Lab billing integration
  • Lab technician dashboards

7. Inventory/Asset Management

  • Master catalog of hospital items (equipment, consumables)
  • Stock level monitor (real-time)
  • Procurement module (purchase orders, vendor quotes)
  • Issue/return logs
  • Expiry tracking
  • Asset maintenance logs
  • Department-wise stock distribution
  • Usage audit
  • Alerts for low stock or expiring items
  • Barcode/QR code tagging

8. Room/Bed Management

  • Floor/ward/room type configuration
  • Real-time occupancy dashboard
  • Bed allotment and transfer system
  • Isolation/ICU room status
  • Bed cleaning & turnaround status
  • Automatic room charge calculation
  • Doctor/Nurse room assignments
  • Alerts for available beds
  • Gender-based/age-based allocation logic
  • Nursing dashboard for patient-wise actionable

9. Reports & Analytics

  • Daily OPD/IPD reports
  • Revenue breakdown (department-wise, doctor-wise)
  • Patient trends (age, disease, region)
  • Inventory consumption reports
  • Lab test volume reports
  • Bed occupancy ratio
  • Staff duty/time log reports
  • Billing summaries (cash, credit, insurance)
  • Compliance reports (HIPAA, NABH)
  • Custom report builder

10. Security & User Access

  • Role-based permission system (admin, doctor, nurse, etc.)
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Password policy enforcement
  • Access logs (who accessed what and when)
  • Data encryption at rest and in transit
  • Audit trails for legal and compliance
  • Backup and disaster recovery module
  • Data export control (CSV, PDF access restrictions)
  • GDPR or HIPAA compliance settings
